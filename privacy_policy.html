Privacy Policy for Time Sage

Effective Date: 01/11/2024

Time Sage ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use the Time Sage mobile application (“App”) on iOS and Android platforms.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, and location.
  • Usage Data: Data related to app functionality, such as clock-in locations.

2. Data Collection Methods

Data is collected through user input and device permissions, only when necessary for specific features.

3. Purpose of Data Collection

We use your data solely to provide the App’s core features, including time tracking, scheduling, and other functionalities.

4. Information Sharing

We may share limited information with trusted third parties:

  • RevenueCat: For managing in-app subscriptions.
  • Google Places API: To support location-based features.
  • Firebase: Used for notifications.

We do not sell or distribute your data for any other purpose.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Our servers are hosted securely on AWS and MongoDB Cloud to ensure data integrity and confidentiality.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data until you choose to delete your account. Upon deletion, all associated data will be permanently removed.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request data deletion. You can delete your account directly through the app or request deletion by visiting our Data Deletion Request Form.

8. Data Transfer

We do not transfer your personal data internationally. All data remains securely within our controlled infrastructure.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. You can access the latest version at any time via the App’s Settings.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us through in-app support tickets or by emailing us at businesscodepulse@gmail.com.
